the courage to be seen

April 25, 2024 -

corny ahh title, i know - but stick with me here

this first post has a few different ideas and origins ive gathered from the last few years

swxy’s learn in public

through a colleague of mine i ended up hearing about swxy and more importantly his learn in public mentality. its a really cool rule and makes total sense, but man

looking dumb is hard

or at least i think it is. i dont even have an audience who will see what im learning, but stepping outside my head where its just “man im so fucking smart and cool and well endowed”, is tough

comfort zone

good segue (seg ooey) into comfort zone stuff - ive watched and read all the jocko videos and goggins books about carry the boats this and “good” that, which is great and works pretty well for physical activities, but intellectual ones are so much harder for me

i think this could work for a “do something that makes you uncomfortable” everyday type gig

or at least until it becomes comfortable to talk into the void like this

writers vs readers

i saw a tweet about how the writers you know are much cooler than the readers you know - and its kinda true

i would expand “writers” to any creative vs people who just study w/e the thing is, but it does checkout for me at least

its obviously not always true and all that but i think it has something to do with the courage to be seen (wayo title plug) and the type of person you have to be to put yourself out there

exposing yourself to critisism in your art is a vulnerable position to be in and takes big cajones

maybe mine will grow

habits and accountability

this video made me buy all 3 books and read em, and he wasnt lying theyre all really fucking good

i guess this is my attempt at applying these learnings to something more general like “here are my thoughts today” instead of a gym routine or some wake up early type shit

the crippling idea that i could look like a dickhead if i stop posting after claiming to start some grand routine will also help keep me accountable

building personal image

this ones kinda more “thats probably a good idea” than any esoteric goal, but engaging with people and having some sort of pesudo 3rd place sounds nice to me

its also a great way to have an audience to shill my app/product/shitty saas builds to :smirk:

please give me money

thats my long winded introduction for why im starting this, will hopefully try to keep this to some sort of schedule
