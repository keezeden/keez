corny ahh title, i know - but stick with me here

this first post has a few different ideas and origins ive gathered from the last few years

### swxy's [learn in public](https://www.swyx.io/learn-in-public)

through a [colleague](https://github.com/chopfitzroy) of mine i ended up hearing about swxy and more importantly his learn in public mentality. its a really cool rule and makes total sense, but man

looking dumb is hard

or at least i think it is. i dont even have an audience who will _see_ what im learning, but stepping outside my head where its just "man im so fucking smart and cool and well endowed", is tough

### comfort zone

good segue (seg ooey) into comfort zone stuff - ive watched and read all the [jocko](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jocko_Willink) videos and [goggins](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/David_Goggins) books about [carry the boats](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OKN8dFO_ZLA) this and ["good"](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IdTMDpizis8) that, which is great and works pretty well for physical activities, but intellectual ones are so much harder for me

i think this could work for a ["do something that makes you uncomfortable"](/forgor) everyday type gig

or at least until it becomes comfortable to talk into the void like this

### writers vs readers

i saw [a tweet](/forgor) about how the writers you know are much cooler than the readers you know - and its kinda true

i would expand "writers" to any creative vs people who just study w/e the thing is, but it does checkout for me at least

its obviously not always true and all that but i think it has something to do with the courage to be seen (wayo title plug) and the type of person you have to be to put yourself out there

exposing yourself to critisism in your art is a vulnerable position to be in and takes big cajones

maybe mine will grow

### habits and accountability

[this video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mqIwwomreJ8) made me buy all 3 books and read em, and he wasnt lying theyre all really fucking good

the crippling idea that i could look like a dickhead if i stop posting after claiming to start some grand routine will also help keep me accountable
